About Us

We are fortunate to have a wonderful staff that has been committed to making Firehouse Pet Shop a great place to come and shop. Together our team has earned the following recognitions: Wenatchee Worlds "Worlds Best" Pet Store in 2016, 2017, 2018, 2019 and 2020 "Friendliest Staff" Wenatchee Business Journal Readers Choice Awards 2015 Winner "Best New Business" 2015 Wenatchee Downtown Association

We also wanted to have all your Dog and Cat needs covered under one roof including; Healthy food options, Supplies, Self-Wash Spa, and Full Service Grooming. We love finding unique items you can't find in a big box store. When you come into the shop we want you and your furry family members to feel absolutely spoiled with attention! A Highlight of our shop is our adoption room, in which we partner with the Wenatchee Valley Humane Society! It was an absolute no-brainer for us to open up in the downtown core, it is amazing to feel the heartbeat and vibe of the area, one that is very hard to find in a strip mall for example. We are truly blessed and grateful for all the support from our family, staff, and our amazing customers!
